Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Home

Description

Not much to say on this one, in fact its just like the fried green tomatoes. A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a sandwich with peanut butter and jelly inside it.

I ate these so much as a kid that I got sick of them, then grew back into them later in life.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Peanut Butter of your choice
  • Jelly of your choice

Steps

  1. Lay out your two slices of bread
  2. Get some of that peanut butter on your knife and spread it on one slice of bread.
  3. Scrape residual peanut butter off the knife back into the peanut butter container, then what little is left on the knife can be wiped onto the empty slice of bread.
  4. Get some of that jelly on your knife and spread it on the other slice of bread.
  5. Slap those two slices together
  6. Enjoy
